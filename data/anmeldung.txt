What is the Anmeldung?
Updated on March 15, 2026
By
Nicolas Bouliane
In Germany, you must register your address at the
Bürgeramt
. This will become your official address in Germany. This is called the
Anmeldung
.
When you do your
Anmeldung
, you get…
A registration certificate (
Anmeldebestätigung
)
A tax ID (
Steueridentifikationsnummer
)
In Berlin, you can do your Abmeldung online or in person. You can also
pay someone to do it for you
.
