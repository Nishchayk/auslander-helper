Video call with a doctor
A video call is faster and easier than a doctor visit. It’s a good way to get a
sick note
. Your employer might need a sick note if you take
sick leave
.
The
TeleClinic
and
Doktor.de
apps let you schedule a video call with a doctor. It’s only in German, but some doctors speak English.
This service is free. Your
public health insurance
pays for it. Your
private health insurance
reimburses it.
