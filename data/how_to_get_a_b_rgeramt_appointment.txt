How to get a Bürgeramt appointment in Berlin
Updated on
June 3, 2026
By
Nicolas Bouliane
This guide explains how to get an appointment at the
Bürgeramt
in Berlin.
You might not need an appointment.
For example, you can
register
and
deregister
your address online. You don’t need to go to the Bürgeramt.
How to do your Anmeldung online
How to do your Abmeldung online
Book an appointment online
Choose a service in the
list of services
. For example, for the
Anmeldung
or the
Abmeldung
.
Scroll to the list of locations, and select all locations. You can go to any
Bürgeramt
in Berlin, not just the one near you. You can’t go to a
Bürgeramt
in
Brandenburg
.
Choose an appointment date.
Receive a verification code by email. You have 10 minutes to enter the activation code. If you don’t get an email, try again with a different email address.
7
Enter the verification code in the appointment booking page.
Get an appointment confirmation.
If there are no appointments
Sometimes, there are no appointments available. Try again every few minutes. Most new appointments appear during working hours. There are more appointments in the morning, between 8:00 and 11:00.
1
Look for appointments everywhere in Berlin, not just near you.
The Bürgeramt does not release all available appointments online. Some appointments are only for people who call
115
. Other appointments are only given by Bürgeramt employees.
2
Multiple services, one appointment
You can book a single appointment for multiple services:
Select a
Bürgeramt
from the
list of
Bürgeramt
locations
Scroll to the list of services.
Select multiple services
Click “
An diesem Standort einen Termin buchen
”.

Call 115
Call
115
between 7:00 and 18:00.
3
This is the
Bürgertelefon
. They can find you a
Bürgeramt
appointment, even when there are no appointments online.
2
They only speak German. You must wait a few minutes to talk to someone.
Email the Bürgeramt
Email your local
Bürgeramt
, explain what you need, and ask for an appointment. If your situation is urgent, include a proof. You might get an appointment faster, even if there are no appointments online.
4
They usually reply in 1 or 2 days.
How to contact the Bürgeramt
Go to the Bürgeramt in person
If your situation is urgent, you can go to the
Bürgeramt
in person. They will help you on the same day, or give you an emergency appointment. For example, if you need documents to travel soon, you can go in person.
6
If your situation is not urgent, ask for an appointment by email. It’s faster.
5
How to cancel your appointment
If you can’t go to your appointment, cancel it.
In your confirmation email, there is a link to cancel your appointment.
Click the link, and cancel your appointment.
Bring a translator
Many Bürgeramt employees only speak German. If you don’t speak German, bring a translator or a German-speaking friend. I recommend
Red Tape Translation
.
Appointments for the
Anmeldung
and the
Abmeldung
are simple, so you probably don’t need a translator.
If you sign a
Vollmacht
, someone can go to the
Bürgeramt
for you.
Red Tape Translation
can do it. It’s expensive, but it saves time.
Contact the Bürgeramt
If you have a problem, email the Bürgeramt. They usually reply a few days later. Sometimes it takes a few weeks.
